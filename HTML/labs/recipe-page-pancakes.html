Lemon Ricotta Pancakes

Start your morning with a stack of delicious homemade pancakes! Fresh lemon juice, creamy ricotta, and sweet blueberries make this breakfast a delight for any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking Powder
  • Milk
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Lemon Juice
  • Lemon Zest
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Fresh or Frozen Blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your nonstick skillet or pan to medium heat.
  2. In one bowl, combine the flour and baking powder and mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the eggs, milk, and ricotta.
  4. In another bowl, melt the unsalted butter and mix it with the lemon juice and lemon zest.
  5. Add the butter mixture to the milk mixture and stir to combine.
  6. Pour the wet mixture into the flour mixture and stir. Do not overmix.
  7. Add butter to the pan just before cooking. Ladle batter into the pan and let it cook until the edges begin to bubble.
  8. Flip the pancake over and let it cook for an additional 1-2 minutes. Remove from pan and place onto a plate. Repeat as many times as you'd like (depending on how hungry you are!)
  9. Serve immediately with your favourite toppings such as syrup or blueberries. These pancakes also taste great on their own!
